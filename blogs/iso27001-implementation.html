ISO 27001

Securing Your Business with ISO 27001:2022

ISO 27001 remains the global benchmark for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS). The 2022 updates introduced new controls for cloud security, threat intelligence, and data masking—areas where audit failures now cluster. A robust ISMS reduces breach likelihood by 57% and cuts recovery time by 35% (ENISA 2024).

Common Implementation Challenges

ChallengeRoot CauseTip
Scoping confusion Cloud and SaaS assets excluded unintentionally Run a data-flow workshop to map all asset boundaries.
Control overload Misinterpreting mandatory vs. risk-based controls Use a risk-based Statement of Applicability (SoA) with peer review.
Risk assessment fatigue Overreliance on qualitative heat maps Quantify risks in ₹ or € using the FAIR model and present confidence intervals.
Policy shelf-ware Lengthy templates that stakeholders ignore Create one-page visual SOPs and embed them in onboarding.
Audit panic Last-minute lack of evidence Schedule a mock audit three months prior; track in Kanban.

Our 6-Step Fast-Track Method

  1. Rapid Readiness Scan (Week 1): 40-control checklist aligned to ISO 27001:2022.
  2. Executive Risk Workshop (Week 2): FAIR-based loss scenarios defining risk appetite.
  3. Smart ISMS Build (Weeks 3–8): Cloud-first controls, zero-trust maps, evidence automation.
  4. Human Firewall (Weeks 6–10): Role-based training, phishing drills, culture pulse surveys.
  5. Mock Audit & Review (Weeks 11–12): Certified auditors simulate Stage 1 & 2; auto-generated board pack.
  6. Certification Support & CI (Week 13→): We guide Stage 1 & 2, close non-conformities, and hand over dashboards.
